About Us

Founded in 2008, Stellar Public School stands as one of Bengaluru’s leading educational institutions, committed to nurturing intellectual curiosity, creativity, and moral values in students. Our mission is to create confident, compassionate, and future-ready citizens who thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Vision & Mission

  • Vision: To empower students with knowledge, empathy, and global perspective.
  • Mission: To provide a balanced education combining academics, arts, sports, and life skills.
“Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think.” – Albert Einstein

Academic Programs

Early Years (Nursery – KG)

Play-based exploration, phonics, and social-emotional learning form the foundation of early education.

Primary (Grades 1–5)

Focus on literacy, numeracy, and science concepts through inquiry-based projects and activity-driven learning.

Secondary (Grades 6–10)

STEM-driven learning, coding, lab work, and cross-disciplinary projects strengthen analytical skills.

Senior Secondary (Grades 11–12)

Comprehensive preparation for JEE, NEET, and other competitive exams with personalized mentorship and career guidance.

Subjects Offered

  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science)
  • Commerce (Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, Informatics Practices)
  • Humanities (History, Geography, Political Science, Psychology)
